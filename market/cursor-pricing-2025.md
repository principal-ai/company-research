# Cursor usage-based pricing change and backlash (2025)

Note on sourcing: the pricing change and apology are documented by the company;
the surrounding detail here is from trade/community coverage (secondary). One
aspect per heading.

## The change
In June 2025, Cursor replaced request caps with usage-based billing pegged to
underlying model API pricing. The prior Pro plan was $20/month for 500 "fast
requests"; under the new model, heavier models, longer contexts, and MAX mode
consume more of the included amount.

## The backlash
The rollout was widely seen as poorly communicated — users reported finding their
plan effectively changed without clear notice. Workflows like large refactors and
long-context conversations began triggering unexpected overage charges, with some
users posting daily charges of $10–$20.

## Company response
Cursor issued a public apology on 2025-07-04 and offered refunds for unexpected
charges incurred between 2025-06-16 and 2025-07-04. A portion of users reportedly
migrated to alternatives such as Windsurf.
