# Stack Overflow 2025 Developer Survey — AI findings

Annual survey of developers. Figures below are from the 2025 edition (results
released 2025-12-29). One finding per heading.

## Adoption still rising
84% of respondents use or plan to use AI tools in their development process, up
from 76% in 2024.

## Trust declining
29% of respondents trust the accuracy of AI output; 46% actively distrust it —
up from 31% distrust in 2024. Only 3% report "high trust."

## Experienced developers most skeptical
Experienced developers report the lowest "highly trust" rate (2.6%) and the
highest "highly distrust" rate (20%).

## "Almost right but not quite"
66% report that AI answers are "almost right, but not quite."

## Time lost debugging AI output
45% say they lose significant time debugging AI-generated code.

## Human verification remains the fallback
The most-cited reason developers still ask a person for help is "when I don't
trust AI's answers" (75%).
