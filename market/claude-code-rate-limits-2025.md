# Claude Code weekly rate limits and lawsuit (2025)

One aspect per heading.

## The change
In late August 2025, Anthropic added weekly rate limits for Claude Pro and Max
subscribers, on top of the existing five-hour rolling limits. Anthropic said the
change affected fewer than 5% of subscribers, targeting continuous Claude Code
use and policy violations such as account sharing and reselling.

## The limits
Reported allotments: Pro users get roughly 40–80 hours of Sonnet 4 per week;
$100 Max subscribers get roughly 140–280 hours of Sonnet 4 plus 15–35 hours of
Opus 4.

## The backlash
Developers objected that the limits punish many users for the behavior of a few,
and asked for transparency on what changed, who is affected, and how limits are
measured.

## The lawsuit
A class-action suit was filed over the Max usage limits, seeking class status for
buyers of Max 5x and Max 20x since those tiers launched in April 2025, and asking
for refunds and damages under consumer-protection law.
