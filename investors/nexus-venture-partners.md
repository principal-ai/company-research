# Nexus Venture Partners

**Nexus Venture Partners** is an early-stage **US–India** venture capital firm. It
partners with founders from inception through Series A (and beyond), with a
two-track focus: **enterprise software in the US** and **digitally-enabled
businesses in India**. Offices in **Menlo Park** (Sand Hill Road), **Mumbai**, and
**Bengaluru**.

## Overview

- **Firm:** Nexus Venture Partners; offices Menlo Park, Mumbai, Bengaluru.
- **AUM:** ~$3.2B _(third-party, Tracxn/Crunchbase — re-verify)_.
- **Stage:** Inception / Seed / Series A (also pre-seed and Series B per third-party profiles).
- **Check size:** ~$100K–$10M _(third-party — re-verify)_.
- **Sectors:** Enterprise tech / SaaS, AI/ML, data infrastructure, dev tools, data security, fintech, open source — plus India consumer/digital businesses.
- **Geography:** US (enterprise software) and India (digital businesses).

## Thesis & what they value

- **Two-track early-stage.** US enterprise software + India digital — early and hands-on, partnering through the company lifecycle.
- **AI / data infrastructure.** A current through-line: AI/ML, data infrastructure, agentic workflows, distributed systems, and developer tools.

## Stated investment criteria (per investor card, 2026-06-19)

- **Investment focus:** Early-stage (Seed / Series A) B2B / enterprise software, data, and edge AI.
- **Comfort / maturity:** MVP / Beta.
- **Rounds:** Seed.
- **Style:** Can lead.
- **Teams:** International teams OK.
