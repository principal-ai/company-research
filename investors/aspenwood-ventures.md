# Aspenwood Ventures

**Aspenwood Ventures** is an early-stage, enterprise-software–focused venture
capital firm in San Francisco. It positions itself as the **"first institutional
capital"** for a select few B2B/SaaS companies and commits long-term by joining
boards. The firm is the **leadership transition out of Hummer Winblad Venture
Partners** — its two Managing Directors, Lars Leckie and Steve Kishi, both came
from Hummer Winblad (the team page carries an endorsement from Ann Winblad).

## Overview

- **Firm:** Aspenwood Ventures, San Francisco, CA.
- **Stage:** Early-stage — "first institutional capital through the long haul." (Pre-Seed, Seed, Series A per our investor card.)
- **Sectors:** Enterprise software, with emphasis on SaaS; some blockchain/infrastructure.
- **Geography:** San Francisco–based; US enterprise software.
- **Check size:** Not stated on the site _(left blank — not guessed)_.
- **Approach:** Backs a small number of companies as first institutional money and joins boards for the long haul; operator-led (both MDs are former founders/engineers).
- **Team:** Lars Leckie (Managing Director); Steve Kishi (Managing Director); Maria Mulcahy (CFO); Haley Etter (Executive Assistant).
- **Notable portfolio (attributed):** Klue, Amberdata, nfinite, NeuVector, Jamm, Aria Systems; Sonatype (per search). Earlier Leckie investments include Aria Systems and Sonatype.

## Thesis & what they value

- **First institutional capital, long-term.** Targets a "select few outstanding companies," leads early and stays on the board through the build.
- **Enterprise software / SaaS focus.** B2B software driving "disruptive innovation."
- **Operator-led partnership.** Both Managing Directors are entrepreneurs and engineers themselves — the pitch is founder-centric mentorship from people who've "raised capital, struggled to meet payroll, managed through down cycles."

## Stated investment criteria (per investor card, 2026-06-19)

- **Investment focus:** Early-stage enterprise software companies.
- **Comfort / maturity:** MVP / Beta.
- **Rounds:** Pre-Seed, Seed, Series A.
- **Style:** Open to both (leading and following).
