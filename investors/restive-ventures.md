# Restive Ventures

**Restive Ventures** is an early-stage, fintech-focused venture capital firm based
in San Francisco. It backs founders "building the future of financial services,"
pairing early capital with an operator-led founder program. (Third-party profiles
note it was earlier known as **Financial Venture Studio**.)

## Overview

- **Firm:** Restive Ventures (a.k.a. Restive), San Francisco, CA. Founded ~2018 (third-party profiles).
- **Stage:** Pre-Seed, Seed, Series A — "Nothing is too early for us."
- **Sectors:** Fintech, Insurtech, AI, Consumer, SaaS — with a fintech / financial-services through-line.
- **Geography:** US-only — "early-stage fintech companies in the US."
- **Check size:** $50K–$2.5M per third-party profiles (vcsheet); **our investor card cites $500k–$5M** (see `note`). Reported as not leading rounds (vcsheet).
- **Partners:** Ryan Falvey (Founder & Managing Partner); Tyler Griffin (Managing Partner); Cameron Peake (Partner).
- **Notable portfolio (attributed):** Propel, Point, Acorns (vcsheet).

## Thesis & what they value

- **Founders in financial services first.** Targets "visionary founders building technology companies that will transform financial services"; leans on operating experience and relationships to help founders through fintech's regulatory and technical complexity.
- **AI for financial services.** Per our investor card, Restive "writes checks for exceptional founders building **AI apps and infra for financial services** at the earliest stage."
- **Operator-led founder program — "Restive Founders."** An accelerator-style network ("an elite network of the best & brightest fintech entrepreneurs") layered on top of the capital — a structured fintech program to help early companies navigate financial-services-specific challenges and scale.
- **Tagline:** "Where founders build the future of financial services." (*restive* = unable to keep still, from impatience or dissatisfaction.)

## Stated investment criteria (per investor card, 2026-06)

- **Comfort / maturity:** Napkin Stage, MVP / Beta, Traction, Revenue.
- **Rounds:** Pre-Seed, Seed.
- **Style:** Open to both (leading and following).
- **Teams:** US-based teams only.

## Contact — Ben Glenney (Associate)

The Restive contact on our pitch-event card; the meeting slot is a 20-min
in-person at 11:50 AM PST.

- **Role:** Associate at Restive Ventures (joined ~July 2025; VCWire).
- **Focus:** Financial services / fintech; early-stage companies showing traction; "markets in flux" with founders who have a personal stake in the problem.
- **Background:** In VC since ~2020; previously Pilatus Capital (Senior Associate), began at CFV Ventures (fintech-focused). Says he's backed 25+ early-stage startups. Based in San Francisco.
- **Education:** BS, Business Administration — USC Marshall School of Business.
- **In his words:** "the best companies aren't just chasing a big idea — they're built by teams who believe in the mission, move fast, and know how to actually get things done together."
