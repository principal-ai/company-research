# Mighty Capital

**Mighty Capital** is a San Francisco venture capital firm built around **product
management** as the edge. Its core thesis is **"the best product wins"** — it
backs B2B technology companies from Seed to Series A and sources/diligences them
through a large network of product leaders (its "Product Alpha" signal).

## Overview

- **Firm:** Mighty Capital, San Francisco, CA.
- **Stage:** Seed to Series A (early-stage).
- **Sectors:** B2B technology / enterprise SaaS, with breadth into security, health AI, fintech, cloud, and blockchain.
- **Edge / sourcing:** A large product-leader community used to spot outliers early via product signals ("best product wins").
- **Check size:** Not recorded _(left blank — not guessed)_.
- **Founders / leadership:** SC Moatti (Founding/Managing Partner; ex-Facebook); Jennifer Vancini (General & Founding Partner); Alan Kramer. Team includes product-focused partners (e.g., Renee Niemi, Naeem Raza).
- **Notable portfolio (attributed):** Airbnb, DigitalOcean (per Tracxn/Crunchbase).

## Thesis & what they value

- **"The best product wins."** Product quality is the leading indicator — Mighty bets on companies whose product itself is the outlier signal, and leans on a product-management lens through diligence and support.
- **Product-led B2B at the early stage.** Seed–Series A B2B/enterprise software, with a community of product leaders as both sourcing channel and post-investment support.

## Stated investment criteria (per investor card, 2026-06-19)

- **Investment focus:** B2B tech and AI.
- **Comfort / maturity:** Traction.
- **Rounds:** Seed.
- **Style:** Open to both (leading and following).
- **Teams:** US-based teams only.
